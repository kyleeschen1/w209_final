Do Democrats or Republicans tend to have higher margins of victory?


Victory is not binary, although in politics the consequences often are. These graphs allow us to investigate margins of victory, data that oftentimes gets lost in public recollection. When Republicans win, how much do they win by? How comfortably do Democrats eclipse their competition?

Exercises

  • Do Democrats or Republicans have higher average MoV as a % of total votes? How about median?

  • Do Democrats or Republicans have higher average MoV in terms of vote count? How about median?

  • Do Republicans or Democrats have more consistent MoVs?

  • How do outliers skew your answers above?
